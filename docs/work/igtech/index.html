Welcome to Inspire Global Technologies

Offshore Recruitment Process Outsourcing, simply put, is the transfer of onshore recruitment process elements to an offshore service provider. Given today’s workforce, almost 95% of the world’s most dynamic employees cannot be reached using conventional methods – as an alternative to the “you find us” technique, it’s imperative to apply the “we’ll find you” system where you can identify a pool of candidates for the right job. For this, however, you need to use the services of a competent Human Resource Outsourcing (HRO) company who will help boost the efficiency and quality of your HR services and contribute to its success. And that’s specifically what we at Inspire Global Technologies do for you!

Based in Mumbai, India Inspire Global Technologies is the leading provider of Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services to the International Recruitment Market.
